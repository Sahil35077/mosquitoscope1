📚 Mosquito basics

Mosquitoes (family Culicidae) are flies with ~3,500 species worldwide. Only females of most species bite — they need blood proteins to develop eggs.

  • Lifespan: 2 weeks – 6 months
  • Wings beat ~500×/second
  • Detect CO₂ from 50+ meters
  • Breed in still freshwater

🧬 Major genera

Aedes Day biters · dengue, Zika, yellow fever
Anopheles Night biters · malaria vectors
Culex Urban pests · West Nile virus
Psorophora Large floodwater species
